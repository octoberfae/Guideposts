Track the Deck

A traveling tarot deck on a journey
from reader to reader

Guideposts is a single physical deck of tarot cards, passed from one set of hands to another. Each recipient spends time with the cards, then sends the deck on its way. Follow the route, and explore the stories it gathers along the way.

“I was surprised how emotional I felt holding the deck. Each card felt alive with purpose, like it had more to say the longer I listened.”

– Ashleigh R., Oregon

The Magician

The Infinite Potential

The heroine of the card is magnetic, brimming with confidence. Her stance with a sword and a magician hat, overseeing transformation, suggests opportunity, motion, and strength.

The Tools

Sword: intellect; communication; petals: signify love, cooperation, light; creative energy. She taunts conduit and works with them.

The Curtain

The vibrant curtain behind the Magician hints at hidden mystery, greater spiritual power, and connections beyond the present plane.

About This Project

This digital companion was created to accompany a traveling tarot deck, offering a way for readers to record their
reflections and follow the deck's path. It is a living, evolving story written by everyone it touches.
