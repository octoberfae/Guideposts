Track the Deck

Guideposts is a single physical deck of tarot cards, passed from one set of hands to another. Each recipient spends time with the cards, then sends the deck on its way. Follow the route, and explore the stories it gathers along the way.

About This Project

This digital companion was created to accompany a traveling tarot deck, offering a way for readers to record their reflections and follow the deck's path. It is a living, evolving story written by everyone it touches.
